Eggy Car: The Egg-Citing Rolling Game

Eggy Car is a hilarious physics-based mobile game where you control wacky vehicles carrying fragile eggs. Your mission? Navigate treacherous terrain without cracking your precious cargo!

How to Play Eggy Car

Tap to accelerate and release to brake as you maneuver your egg-loaded vehicle through challenging courses. Balance speed with caution - go too fast and you all smash your eggs, but too slow and you won't complete the level. Unlock new crazy cars and compete for high scores in this addictive egg-rolling adventure!
