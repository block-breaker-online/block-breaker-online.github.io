Little Alchemy: The Ultimate Element Combining Game

Little Alchemy is a captivating puzzle game where players combine basic elements to discover new items. Start with just four elements - air, earth, fire, and water - then mix them to create hundreds of surprising combinations. The game challenges your creativity as you experiment with different pairings to unlock all possible elements.

To play, simply drag one element onto another. If they can combine, you'll create something new! Discover everything from animals to advanced technology in this addictive alchemy simulator. Perfect for casual gamers and puzzle lovers alike.
