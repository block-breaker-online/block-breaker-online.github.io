Retro Bowl College: Relive Classic Football Action

Retro Bowl College brings nostalgic pixel-style football to your fingertips! This addictive game lets you build your dream college team, strategize plays, and compete for championships in a retro-inspired format.

How to Play Retro Bowl College:

1. Build your roster by recruiting star players
2. Call plays using simple swipe controls
3. Score touchdowns with precision passing/running
4. Manage your program through seasons of NCAA-style action

Experience the perfect blend of arcade fun and strategic depth!
