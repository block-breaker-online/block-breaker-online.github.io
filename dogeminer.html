Doge Miner: Mine Dogecoins and Become a Crypto Billionaire!

Doge Miner is an addictive idle clicker game where you mine Dogecoins, upgrade your tools, and hire Shiba Inus to work for you. Start small by clicking the moon to mine coins, then invest in upgrades to automate your mining operation. The goal? Become the richest Dogecoin tycoon in the universe!

How to Play Doge Miner:

  1. Click the moon to mine Dogecoins manually
  2. Buy upgrades to increase your mining power
  3. Hire Doge helpers to mine automatically
  4. Unlock new planets with higher value coins
  5. Prestige to earn special bonuses and mine faster

Pro tip: The more you play, the more your mining operation grows! Check back often to collect your coins and expand your Dogecoin empire.
